The Covanent

Who are the Covanent

The Covanent is a group of 8 diffrent alien races, attempting to find all Forerunner artifacts. The Covanent declared a war on humanity and all of the human colonies when the UNSC resisted the Covanent take over of a human colony with a forrunner artifact on it.
