Huragok

Who are the Huragok

The Huragok are commonly known as Engineers

The Huragok are support creatures. They are Artificial Lifeforms created by the Forerunners and enslaved by the Covanent .

The Engineers help both the Covanent and the UNSC. They have a positive area effect.
