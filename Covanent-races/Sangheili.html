Who are the Sangheili

The Sangheili are commonly known as the Elites

The Elites were known for being the higherarchy of the Covanent military. Until the Prophet of Truth order the genocide of the Eliets.

Once they were exciled from the Covanent they joined forced with the UNSC.

The home world of the Elites is known as Sangheilos which is where the Sangheili's got their name.
